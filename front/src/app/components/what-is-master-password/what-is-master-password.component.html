What is a Master Password?

A Master Password is a primary password that you use to protect access to all your sensitive data. In the context of our password management application, the Master Password is used to encrypt and decrypt your personal information.

Without your Master Password, no one can access your data, not even us. This means you are the only one who can decrypt and view your passwords and sensitive information.

Why is a Master Password important?

The Master Password adds an extra layer of security. Even if someone gains access to your account, they cannot see your passwords without the Master Password.

Security Tips

  • Choose a strong and unique Master Password that you do not use anywhere else.
  • Never share your Master Password with anyone.
  • Use a complex passphrase with uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters.
  • Change your Master Password regularly to maximize security.

Remember:

Your Master Password is the key to your vault. If you forget it, you will not be able to access your stored data. Always keep it safe and secure.

How does encryption work?

When you enter your Master Password, the application uses advanced encryption algorithms to decrypt your data. This means your information is always stored securely and cannot be read without the Master Password.
